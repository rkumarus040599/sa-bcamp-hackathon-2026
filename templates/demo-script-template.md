# Demo Script

## 1. Hook

Open with the customer pain and the business impact in less than 30 seconds.

## 2. Before

Show what the current state looks like or explain the failure clearly.

## 3. During

Walk through the key product or architecture moment that proves the value.

## 4. After

Show the outcome, metric, or saved effort.

## 5. Close

Tie the solution back to why it matters and what happens next.
